# Logistics Platform

This project is a comprehensive logistics platform built with PHP.

## Features (Planned)

- Company Details Management
- Integration Configuration (Zid, Salla, CITC, SMS)
- Shipment Management (All Orders, Bulk Forward, Manual Forward, Reverse Orders, etc.)
- Inventory Management
- Stock Location Management
- Item Management
- Vehicle Management
- Box Management
- Return Manifest Management
- Warehouse Management
- Manifest Management
- Ticket Management
- Offer Management
- Pickers Management
- Packaging Management
- Dispatching Management
- Seller Management
- User Management
- Finance Management
- Route Management
- Courier Service Management
- Location Management
- FAQ Management
- Reports Management
- Access Management
- Logging
